Our Services

Computer Tune up

includes physical dusting of the system; checking for overheating and or damaged components; virus and spyware checking and removal; removing unnecessary files, processes and programs to speed up the computer; updating drivers, windows components and antivirus programs; addressing specific concerns of the customer.

Price starting at $99. Average turnaround 1 to 2 business days

Computer Reload

includes back up of the customer data( usually documents, pictures, music), testing the hard drive if necessary, loading Windows, drivers, antivirus, Windows updates, transferring the customer data back onto the new load etc.

Price starting at $99. Average turnaround 1 to 2 days

Data transfer or back-up

Data recovery
