About us

Pier Street Computers has been offering efficient and highly qualified computer services to Campbell River and the North Island since 1999. We are locally owned and operated and have competitive pricing on both computer sales and services.

We build the desktop computers we sell here on the premises which give our customers the following of advantages:

  • Most warranty work is handled here on site within a day or two, not weeks
  • We can custom build a computer according to the specs the customer desires.
  • We can give a good advice to the customer about the configuration that best suits their particular computer needs and build the computer accordingly
  • Advise the customer on the most cost effective computer solution for their needs
  • The customers benefit from the extensive knowledge that building the computers in our store gives us
  • Excellent upgradeability of the computers we build
  • The customer can save some of the cost of a new tower by getting his current one upgraded instead
